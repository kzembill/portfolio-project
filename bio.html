Hi, my name is Kaliope Caras

Entrepeneur, based in Ohio USA

Both creative and analytical parts of me are better expressed in coding. I am able to have a vision and execute that vision for the world to see, how cool! I grew up the middle child and only girl of a pretty strict Greek family. I was programmed to conform. There are parts of that upbringing that have benefitted me and parts that have caused me to abandon who I am. In my 34th year of life, I am now exploring all of the options I want to explore. After all, we only live once!

MY DREAM

I want become a hub for the "lost" souls. I have had so many people influence me in life. I attribute much of who I am to my experiences both good and not so good. Without hardship, we may never appreciate the ease. I want to take people on trips in paradise to explore different environements, immerse themselves in the food, people, and lifestyle. Our brains, hearts and souls will thank us for this later. Tours of Greece tied in with wellness/self-discovery, what could be better?!
